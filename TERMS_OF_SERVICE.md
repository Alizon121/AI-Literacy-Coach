## 2. Description of the Service

AI Literacy Coach is a browser extension that observes the prompts you write on supported AI platforms and offers real-time coaching suggestions to help you communicate more effectively with AI systems. Its purpose is educational — to help you learn how to write clearer, more useful prompts.

The Extension does not modify, intercept, or interfere with the AI platform's responses. It only observes input text you type and optionally replaces it if you choose to apply a suggestion.

---

## 3. How the Service Works — On-Device vs. Cloud Processing

Understanding how your data moves is important. The Extension operates in one of two modes depending on your configuration:

### 3a. Local Mode (Ollama)

When configured to use a locally installed model (e.g., Ollama with phi4-mini):

- Your prompts are processed **entirely on your device**.
- No prompt text is transmitted over the internet.
- No data leaves your machine.

This mode provides the strongest privacy guarantee. It requires you to install and run a local AI model separately.

### 3b. Cloud Mode (Groq API)

When configured to use a cloud-based API (e.g., Groq):

- Your prompts are **sent over the internet to Groq's servers** for evaluation.
- The developer (Alizon121) does **not** receive, store, or log your prompt text at any point.
- Groq processes your prompt according to their own [Privacy Policy](https://groq.com/privacy-policy/) and Terms of Service.
- If you supply your own Groq API key, your usage is governed directly by your agreement with Groq.

By using Cloud Mode, you acknowledge that your prompt text is transmitted to a third-party service (Groq) and is subject to their data handling practices.

## 4. Data We Collect and Store

The Extension stores the following data **locally in your browser only** using Chrome's `storage.sync` API:

| Data | Purpose | Where stored |
|---|---|---|
| Usage counts (prompts evaluated, suggestions made/accepted) | In-extension stats display | Local browser storage |
| Extension settings (sensitivity, coaching on/off, trigger delay, etc.) | Personalization | Local browser storage (synced across your Chrome devices via your Google account) |

**We do not collect, transmit, or have access to:**
- The content of any prompt you type or submit
- Your browsing history
- Any personally identifiable information
- Any data from AI platform responses

Settings synced via Chrome's `storage.sync` are handled by Google's infrastructure under your Google account's privacy terms.

## 5. AI Variability Disclaimer

The coaching suggestions generated by the Extension are produced by an AI model. **AI models are inherently non-deterministic.** This means:

- The same prompt evaluated at different times may produce different suggestions.
- Suggestions may occasionally be imperfect, incomplete, or not applicable to your specific use case.
- The Extension does not guarantee that applying a suggestion will improve the outcome of your interaction with any AI platform.

Suggestions are provided for educational purposes only. You are always in control — no changes are made to your prompt unless you explicitly click "Apply."
